Outreach.io Mastery

Learn how to use Outreach for sequencing, prospecting, forecasting, and call intelligence — and how it connects with Salesforce to keep everything in sync.

What Is Outreach — and Why Do We Use It?

Outreach is Actionstep's sales engagement platform. It sits on top of Salesforce and handles the things Salesforce wasn't built for: multi-touch sequencing, email open/click tracking, call intelligence, meeting booking, and pipeline forecasting. Think of Salesforce as your system of record and Outreach as the engine that drives activity against it.

Sequences — Your Automated Multi-Touch Playbook

A Sequence is a pre-built series of touchpoints — emails, calls, LinkedIn steps, tasks — that Outreach executes on a schedule. You enroll a prospect and Outreach handles the pacing, reminders, and tracking. High-performing reps don't wing their outreach — they run structured sequences and refine what works.

Kaia — Your AI Meeting Assistant

Kaia is Outreach's built-in AI that joins your calls, takes notes, surfaces content in real time, and gives you a full transcript after. It's not a nice-to-have — it's how you ensure nothing important is missed and how your manager can coach you on real conversations.

Forecasting in Outreach

Forecasting has moved from the old "Commit" tool directly into Outreach under Opportunities. This is where you manage your Month and Quarter Forecast categories — the same categories that sync back to Salesforce. Get comfortable here; you'll use it every week.

Setup, Mailboxes & Salesforce Sync

Outreach only works if it's properly connected. Before you send your first sequence, you need to configure your mailbox, connect Salesforce, verify Kaia, and set up your calendar. Do these on Day 1 — none of your activity will sync otherwise.
