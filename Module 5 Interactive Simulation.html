🎯 What is the ICP?

Actionstep's Ideal Customer Profile is a business-minded law firm with 20–50 users, operating within your assigned territory, that has a strong administrative layer (Practice Manager, Operations Manager, or IT Manager) with real purchasing influence.

"Workflow = consistency + efficiency = profit" — this is how they think.

🎯 The Four Customer Segments

SegmentWho They ArePriorityHow to Win
Mid-Market Moderns 20–75 users, B2C boutiques, growth-focused, tech-forward. Believe process = profit. #1 Target Sell modern firm vision + workflow as competitive advantage
Established Players 40–200 users, mature firms, feel pressure to modernize, often on-prem switchers. #2 Target Create urgency — cloud, security, peer proof, talent attraction
Multi-Tasking Solos 1–19 users, overwhelmed small firms needing quick wins. High post-sale service cost. Selective Inbound only. Short cycle. Confirm implementation commitment.
Survivors Resistant to change. Won't invest. Noisy and high churn risk. Not Target Do not pursue. Protect your time.
