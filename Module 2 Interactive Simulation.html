Forecast vs. Stretch Yes — The Key Distinction

✓ FORECAST (I THINK)

  • Buyer is engaged and responding to outreach
  • No unresolved blockers standing in the way
  • Pricing reviewed — no objection raised
  • Both sides aligned on timeline and next steps
  • Clear, documented path to close

⚡ STRETCH YES (I HOPE)

  • You are driving — buyer has not confirmed close date
  • No formal next step confirmed from buyer's side
  • Timing uncertain — could slip to next quarter
  • Pricing has not been formally reviewed
  • Working to accelerate — outcome not guaranteed
