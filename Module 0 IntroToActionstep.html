Chapter 1 of 4 · Our Mission & Why

Law firms provide an essential service to our society. Lawyers protect people and assets, reduce collective risk, and help businesses grow. And they deserve technology that helps them do that work — powerfully, efficiently, and on their own terms.

— The Actionstep Purpose

We Think Lawyers Are Awesome

That's not a marketing line. It's the genuine belief that started this company.

📧 An email from our founders to the global team — 2019

Think about the major milestones in your life — good and bad. Buying a home. Settling a dispute. Starting a business. Protecting your family. They are all enabled and eased by what lawyers do.

Lawyers sometimes get a raw deal. Yes, they are meticulous, they like control, they choose their words carefully and love to be right — but we know these super smart professionals are passionate about their client's success above all else. And WE are all their clients at one time or another.

What's holding them back? Poorly defined processes, clunky technology, and the distractions of "the busy-ness" of running a law firm. That's where we come in.

🎯 Our Mission

Actionstep provides powerful technology that enables law firms to thrive as essential advocates, advisors, and businesses in our society.

👤 Who We Do It For

Midsize law firms who want to modernize and systemize how they practice law — ambitious firms with a clear idea about where they want to go.

Problems We Solve

We built Actionstep around the real, recurring frustrations of law firm management. Click each to see how we solve it.

🕳️ Data silos — things fall through the cracks ▼
Actionstep's solution

One connected platform centralizes all data, processes, and tasks. Enter data once — it flows correctly through documents, matters, and communications everywhere. No more switching between separate systems.

⏳ Hours lost to repetitive admin work ▼
Actionstep's solution

Actionstep automates repetitive tasks through powerful workflow tools — email updates, document creation, task routing — that run without manual intervention. Your team focuses on billable, meaningful work.

📉 No visibility into how time and profit are flowing ▼
Actionstep's solution

Advanced reporting and data views give firms real insight into team utilization, billing efficiency, and practice area profitability. Run any report in seconds — no manual data exports needed.

📏 Inconsistent client experience across the team ▼
Actionstep's solution

Structured, automated workflows ensure everyone follows the same process every time. Clients get consistent, high-quality experiences regardless of which team member is working their matter.

🔒 Technology that limits — not enables — growth ▼
Actionstep's solution

Cloud-based, subscription model, and open integrations mean firms never outgrow Actionstep. Scaling is as simple as adding a license — no servers, no infrastructure, no multi-year lock-in.
