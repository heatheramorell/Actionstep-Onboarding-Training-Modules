6sense Mastery

Learn how to use 6sense to find accounts that are actively in-market, prioritize your outreach using intent signals, and stop wasting time on firms that aren't ready to buy.

What Is 6sense — and Why Does It Matter?

6sense is an AI-powered account intelligence and intent data platform. It tells you which law firms are actively researching practice management software right now — even before they fill out a form or respond to outreach. The core insight: most of the buying journey happens before a prospect ever talks to a salesperson. 6sense lets you find them during that hidden phase and reach out at exactly the right moment.

Intent Signals — What 6sense Is Listening For

6sense aggregates signals from across the web — not just your own website — to build a picture of which accounts are in an active research mode. These signals are invisible to you without a tool like 6sense, which is exactly why it creates a competitive advantage. The more signals an account shows, the hotter they are.

The 6sense Buying Stage Model

6sense classifies every account into one of six buying stages based on their intent signal strength and recency. These stages are not the same as your Salesforce opportunity stages — they represent where the buyer is in their own decision process, before and after they engage with you. Knowing the stage tells you how urgently to act and what message to lead with.
